# Mnemo Notebook Mark Similarity Review

Date: 2026-07-10
Scope: Pre-approval visual review of the N-A Notebook mark. This is a product-design comparison, not trademark or legal clearance.

## Mark under review

Mnemo N-A is a single-color closed pocket notebook: a soft rectangular cover outline, a filled vertical elastic band, and a short external ribbon marker. The proposed app-icon presentation uses an olive mark on a pale sage tile; dark and monochrome presentations preserve the same geometry.

## Comparison set

The review covered current note, journal, writing, and personal-knowledge products whose icon territory could plausibly overlap:

- [Apple Notes](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/write-and-draw-in-documents-iph1ac0b3a2/ios): yellow-and-white ruled note sheet; no closed cover, elastic, or ribbon construction.
- [Goodnotes](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/goodnotes-ai-notes-docs-pdf/id1444383602): handwriting strokes and a stylus; materially different silhouette and product signal.
- [Notability](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/notability-smarter-ai-notes/id360593530): pencil/microphone symbol on blue; materially different silhouette.
- [Zoho Notebook](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/notebook-ai-notes-to-do/id973801089): overlapping colored note cards; different multi-object construction and palette.
- [Bear](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/bear-markdown-notes/id1016366447): animal silhouette on red; no geometric overlap.
- [Day One](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/day-one-journal-private-diary/id1044867788): a white bookmark on cyan. The ribbon idea is adjacent, but the complete Mnemo notebook construction is distinct.
- [Agenda](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/agenda-notes-meets-calendar/id1370289240): calendar/timeline-led identity; no meaningful silhouette overlap.
- [Notebooks: Write and Organize](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/notebooks-write-and-organize/id1490084838): the closest comparison. Its icon uses a page/notebook with a blue spine and ruled-paper detail.

## Risk assessment

Overall similarity risk: **moderate concept risk, low direct-rendering risk**.

The notebook metaphor is common and cannot itself create a distinctive identity. “Notebooks: Write and Organize” is the closest neighboring icon because it also presents a notebook/page with a vertical spine. Mnemo differs through:

- a closed cover rather than an open or ruled page;
- a separate full-height elastic band;
- a short external ribbon marker;
- a one-color flat silhouette;
- olive/sage default presentation rather than blue and white;
- no text lines, page layers, pen, sparkle, chat bubble, or AI symbol.

N-B is less distinctive because removing the elastic leaves a generic cover-plus-bookmark silhouette. N-C improves in-app evidence association but the two-color ribbon is less suitable as the primary app-icon master.

## Recommendation

Keep **N-A** as the lead review candidate. Do not promote N-B to the primary mark unless small-size testing proves the elastic unusable. Keep N-C for optional in-content evidence moments only.

Production decision:

The user approved the Sage & Olive direction and asked for the brief, all screens, logos, and icons to be implemented on the redesign branch. N-A is therefore installed for branch review. This is design approval, not trademark clearance.
